Statement

Practice and methodology

David Byrne is an Irish artist currently living and working in southern Japan. He draws inspiration from his physical and cultural surroundings while conceiving conceptual variegations of persistent themes that have preoccupied his imagination for several decades.

His focus of expression encompasses the union of traditional craft and state of the art cutting edge technologies. Currently his efforts explore the conflation of traditional metal casting techniques with 3D design technology such as computer modelling and 3D printing of elements.

His conceptual preoccupations explore entropic principles that manifest as metaphors for transience, order, creation, destruction, chaos and ultimate regeneration. For the artist this infers the implication for a symbolic spiritual consolidation and reconciliation with humanity's common existential experience, Art.
